## 从句
---
### 1. 从句概括
```c
从句可以当：
主语、宾语
主补(表语)、宾补
定语、状语
同位语

名词从句 ---> 主语、宾语、主补(表语)、宾补、同位语
形容词从句/定语从句/关系从句 ---> 定语
副词从句 ---> 状语（时间地点条件等等）

（从句跟在先行词后，但是它在句中哪个位置都是有可能的）
（例如：修饰主语的话则在中间，修饰宾语则跑到后面）
```
### 2. 从句 - 名词从句
```c
成分、意义完整(不表示任何意思)：that

成分完整，意义不完整：
表是否 whether/if
表副词 when/where/why/how

成分不完整：
人：who、whom、whoever、whomever 无论谁
物：what
--------------------------------------------------
主语从句：
(That the rabbit ate the carrot) is obvious. 兔子显然吃了胡萝卜
(Whether the rabbit ate the carrot) is obvious. 兔子是否吃了胡萝卜是显而易见的
(Where the rabbit ate the carrot) is obvious. 兔子吃胡萝卜的地方很明显
(When the rabbit ate the carrot) is obvious. 兔子吃胡萝卜的时候很明显
(How the rabbit ate the carrot) is obvious. 兔子是怎么吃胡萝卜的很明显
(Who ate the carrot) is obvious. 谁吃了胡萝卜是显而易见的
(What the rabbit ate) is obvious. 兔子吃了什么很明显

It 作形式主语：
(That the hungry rabbit had already eaten a carrot for dinner this evening) 
(is obvious). ---> 
(It is obvious)(that the hungry rabbit had already eaten a carrot for dinner 
this evening). It形式，很明显，这只饥饿的兔子今晚晚餐已经吃了一根胡萝卜
--------------------------------------------------
宾语从句：
I saw (that the rabbit ate a carrot). 我看见这兔子吃了根胡萝
I saw (whether the rabbit ate the carrot). 我看见兔子是否吃了胡萝卜
I saw (where the rabbit ate the carrot). 我看到兔子在哪里吃胡萝卜
I saw (when the rabbit ate the carrot). 我看见兔子什么时候吃胡萝卜了
I saw (how the rabbit ate the carrot). 我看到兔子是怎么吃胡萝卜的
I saw (who ate the carrot). 我看见谁吃了胡萝卜
I saw (what the rabbit ate). 我看到兔子吃了什么

that有时可以省略：（多个that、插入语不可省略）
I know that(可省略) the rabbit ate a carrot. 我知道兔子吃了胡萝卜

否定：（在动词前，而不是在从句中）
I (don't think) that the rabbit is smart. 我不认为兔子聪明

主从时态一致：（除非是客观现象）
I (don't) think that (you're) right. 主从都是现在
I (didn't) think that (you were) right. 主从都是过去
--------------------------------------------------
表语从句：（主要是be动词和系动词）

The problem is (that the rabbit is hungry). 问题是兔子饿了
The reason is (that there's no more carrot). 原因是没有胡萝卜了
The question is (whether the rabbit is hungry). 问题是兔子是否饿了
The question is (where the rabbit should eat the carrot). 问题是兔子应该在哪里吃胡萝卜
The question is (when the rabbit should eat the carrot). 问题是兔子应该什么时候吃胡萝卜
The question is (how the rabbit should eat the carrot). 问题是兔子应该怎么吃胡萝卜
The question is (who should eat the carrot). 问题是谁应该吃胡萝卜
The question is (what the rabbit should eat). 问题是兔子应该吃什么

系动词：（be动词、感官、持续、表象、变化）
be动词：am is are was were
感官动词：look、sound、taste、feel、smell + adj
持续系动词：keep、remain、stay
表像系动词：seem、appear
变化系动词：become
go       go bad（变坏）
turn     turn yellow（变颜色）
come     come true（变真）
get      get better（变得更）
fall     fall asleep（睡着）

It seems that the rabbit is hungry. 兔子好像饿了
lt feels that you're not telling the truth. 感觉你没有说实话
--------------------------------------------------
同位语从句：（主要抽象名词，对其解释说明）

fact，news，information，idea，doubt，hope
conclusion 结论，evidence 证据，thought 想法，promise 承诺

The fact that the rabbit ate the carrot did not surprise me. 
兔子吃了胡萝卜这个事实并没让我吃惊
```
### 3. 从句 - 形容词从句
```c
先行词：就是要被形容的 名词
关系词：看先行词是个人还是物，有选关系代词、关系副词
--------------------------------------------------
关系代词构成：
人：that、who、whom、whose
物：that、which（可以互换，that指限定一种选择，which指可以有多种选择）

The rabbit is eating (a carrot that I bought). 兔子正在吃我买的胡萝卜
(The teacher who ate a carrot) is a rabbit. 吃胡萝卜的老师是一只兔子
(The teacher whom I saw yesterday) is a rabbit. 我昨天见到的那位老师是只兔子
(The teacher whose favorite food is carrot) is a rabbit. 最喜欢吃胡萝卜的老师是一只兔子
(The food which the teacher likes) is carrot. 老师喜欢的食物是胡萝卜

关系副词构成：
when --> on which
where --> at which
why --> for which

This was (the place where the rabbit ate the carrot). 这是兔子吃胡萝卜的地方
This is (the reason why the rabbit ate the carrot). 这就是兔子吃胡萝卜的原因
That was (the day when the rabbit ate the carrot for the firsttime). 
那是兔子第一次吃胡萝卜的日子
--------------------------------------------------
非限定形容词从句：（which前面为逗号，表示前面说过的人/物，或是整件事）

The rabbit ate the carrot,which was on the table. 
兔子吃了胡萝卜，(特指)在桌子上的
The rabbit ate the carrot,which was not surprising. 
兔子吃了胡萝卜，(特指)这事儿不令人吃惊
```




### 4. 从句 - 副词从句
```c
时间、地点
比较、条件
让步、方式
原因、目的、结果
```

### 4.1 副词从句 - 时间副词从句
```c
1.表时间：before、after、when、while、as、since、until、as soon as、the next time

① before：（在...之前）
The rabbit ate a carrot at home (before the wolf stopped by).
大灰狼串门之前，兔子吃了根胡萝卜

② when/while/as：（刚好/过程中/同时）
The rabbit was eating a carrot (when I reached home).
我到家的时候，兔子在吃胡萝卜
The rabbit was eating a carrot (while I was making a video).
我做视频的过程中，兔子在吃胡萝下
I ate a carrot (as I made the video). 我一边吃胡萝卜一边做视频

③ after：（在...之后）
The rabbit ate a carrot (after the wolf stopped by). 大灰狼串过门之后,兔子吃了根胡萝卜

④ since：（自从，在前面某个时间点开始算）（用完成时）
The rabbit has shared three carrots (since the wolf stopped by).
自从大灰狼来串门，兔子已经分享了三根胡萝卜了

⑤ until/not until：（直到，在之后的某个时间点）
The rabbit waited (until the wolf stopped by). 兔子一直在等,直到大灰狼来串门.
The rabbit (didn't) eat any carrot (until the wolf stopped by). 
兔子直到大灰狼来串门才吃了胡萝卜

⑥ as soon as：（一...就...）
The rabbit will eat a carrot (as soon as the wolf leaves).
大灰狼一离开兔子就要吃一个胡萝卜

⑦ the next time：（下次）
The rabbit will share the biggest carrot (the next time the wolf stops by).
下次大灰狼来串门，兔子会一起分享最大的胡萝卜
```

### 4.2 副词从句 - 地点副词从句
```c
地点连接词：where、wherever 在任何地方、everywhere、anywhere

The rabbit ate the carrot (where he found it). 兔子在找到胡萝卜的地方吃了它
The rabbit will go (wherever he can find a carrot). 兔子会去任何他能找到胡萝卜的地方
The rabbit sees a carrot (everywhere he goes). 兔子走到哪里都能看到胡萝卜
The rabbit can eat a carrot (anywhere he likes). 兔子可以在任何他喜欢的地方吃胡萝卜
```

### 4.3 副词从句 - 比较副词从句
```c
比较：than、as...as、the...the...、the最高级

① than：
Mary works（harder than）John. 玛丽工作比约翰努力
Mary works (a little harder than) John. 玛丽工作比约翰努力一点

② as...as：
He is (not as/so strong as) a young man. 他不如一个年轻人强壮。

③ the...the:
(The more) you eat,(the fatter) you are. 你吃得越多，你就越胖

④ the最高级：
There is no doubt that she is (one of the best) students in our class.
毫无疑问，她是我们班最好的学生之一
```

### 4.4 副词从句 - 条件副词从句
```c
条件：（条件中，从句总是比主句落后一个时间段）
如果：if、provided、in case
unless 除非、as long as 只要

(If you don't like this video), you will not give it a thumbs-up.
如果你不喜欢这个视频,你不会给它点赞
(Provided that my video is excellent),you will give it a thumbs-up.
如果我的视频很棒，你会给它点赞
(In case you don't understand),please comment below.
如果你不明白，请在下方评论
(Unless you like this ideo).you will not give it a thumbs-up.
除非你喜欢这个视频，不然你不会给它点赞
(As long as I make excellent videos).you will remain my follower.
只要我一直做高质量视频,你就会继续关注我
```

### 4.5 副词从句 - 让步副词从句
```c
即使：even if
无论：although、though、even though、as（他们不能跟but一起用）
no matter = Regardless of
（whatever = no matter what）

(Even if) I see a carrot, I will not eat it. 即使我看到了一根胡萝人,我也不会吃它
(No matter) what happens, I will not eat it. 不管发生什么，我也不会吃它
(No matter) who orders me, I will not eat it. 不管谁命令我，我也不会吃它
(No matter) how hungry I am, I will not eat it. 不管我又多饿我也不会吃它
(Regardless of) what kind of video Imake, you will give it a thumbs-up.
不管我做什么样的视频，你都会给我点赞
```

### 4.6 副词从句 - 方式副词从句
```c
方式：as、as if、as though

You spend so much money (as if you were a millionaire). 
你花了这么多钱就好像你是个百万富翁
```

### 4.7 副词从句 - 原因副词从句
```c
原因：because、since、as
（because都可放，since和as只能放在主句前的从句）

(Because this video is excellent), you should give it a thumbs-up. 
因为这视频很棒，所以要点赞
(Since all my videos are excellent), you should give this video athumbs-up.
众所周知，英语兔只出精品,所以要点赞
(As I have thick skin), I'm begging you for a thumbs-up.
显而易见我脸皮很厚，所以求你点赞
---------------------------------
for：（表推测原因，但不是从句主从地位，只是复合句同等地位的）
You must really like me,(for you have given all my videos a thumbs-up).
你一定喜欢我，因为你给所有英语兔视频都点赞了
```

### 4.8 副词从句 - 目的副词从句
```c
目的：in order、so that（so that不能放句首）

(In order that I could finish the video in time),I pulled an all-nighter.
为了及时完成视频,我熬夜不睡(肝视频)
I pulled an all-nighter,(so that I could finishthe video in time).
我熬夜不睡(肝视频)，就为了及时完成视频
```

### 4.9 副词从句 - 结果副词从句
```c
结果是：so that
如此以至于：so...that、such...that
（so后接形容词，such接名词，记住so good就好）

I ate a lot of carrots for lunch (so that I wasn't hungry at all
in the afternoon).
我午饭吃了很多胡萝卜，这样我下午一点也不饿
I ate (so many carrots for lunch)(that I felt sick afterwards).
我吃了那么多胡萝卜，导致(结果是)我之后感到不舒服
It was (such a tasty carrot)(that I ate it all at once).
这是个如此好吃的胡萝卜，以至于(结果)我一下次就吃完了它
```
